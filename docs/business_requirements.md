## Project Purpose
The goal of this dashboard is to provide **Data Observability**. It allows the operations team to monitor the health of automated pipelines and respond quickly to failures that might impact revenue or logistics.

## Target Audience
* **Data Operations Managers:** To track daily success rates and system uptime.
* **DevOps/Data Engineers:** To identify and fix "Critical" alerts in specific workflows.

## Key Questions Answered
1. Which workflow fails the most often?
2. Is there a significant drop in the volume of data processed compared to yesterday?
3. How many critical incidents are currently unresolved?
